Rapid Transit Systems in the Contiguous United States
From 1897 to 2018
Heavy rail rapid transit systems are defined as an electric railway with the capacity to handle a heavy volume of traffic.
The ridership data provided in the map comes from the APTA Public Ridership Report provided quarterly each year. The report contains data for total unlinked transit passenger trips which is defined as a trip on one transit vehicle regardless of the type of fare paid or transfer presented.
